The Letten Pavilion, situated in Zurich in the upper, less developed section of the Letten, is constructed out of recyclable, affordable and lightweight materials. Its aim is to strengthen the already existing structures and community.

The delicate wooden structure sits on screw foundations. Movable and transparent polycarbonate panels slide through the latter and allow the pavilion to adapt and transform based on different needs. The grip attachments, highlighted in a vibrant red, invite users to engage with the panels.

Acoustics experiments and simulations played a key role and strongly shaped the design. Parametrically designed cork panels along the roof and perforation patterns for the polycarbonate elements, enable users to achieve the desired acoustic ambiance inside.
